The problem we solve

Projects are designed from behind a desk.
Impact happens somewhere else.

Enablers fund rigorous, well-formulated projects with capable teams. But execution takes place in complex territories, with stakeholders who weren't always part of the initial diagnosis. Standard monitoring mechanisms, such as reports, baseline surveys, questionnaires, measure whether the project was implemented as planned. But they don't measure whether what was planned makes sense for the people receiving it.

01

Reports track activities but don't capture why something isn't being adopted.

02

Implementing organizations often struggle to see their own blind spots from within.

03

By the time the problem becomes visible, it's too late to correct course without affecting outcomes or budget.

How the service works
